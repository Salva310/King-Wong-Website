A Taste of Tradition

King Wong has been a beloved destination in Marlton, New Jersey for authentic Chinese-American cuisine. From our kitchen to your table, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch special or gathering the whole family for dinner, we bring the warmth of Chinese hospitality to every meal.
